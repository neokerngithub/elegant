About Us

Modern Edge Architects and Engineers Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2081 (2024) as a Private Limited Company registered under the Registration Act. 2063 (2006 AD) in the Office of the Company Registrar, Government of Nepal.
It was established by the group of experties, laying the foundation to become a leading force in engineering company in Nepal, with a comprehensive portfolio covering architectural designs, planning, estimating, interior / landscape designing, property valuation, construction services, etc. across residential, commercial and governmental projects.
